About us

The fast pace of the world we live in leaves little time for extra things we would like to do, like plan events, parties, and social get-togethers. 8EVEs (Endless Vision Events) is ready to fill the need by being available to take on the burden of planning so that our clients can spend time on more important things.

Founded originally on a part-time basis, it is a small business owned by eight career women that mainly focuses on weddings, birthdays and debut but also provides services for other events like anniversaries, bridal showers, christening, corporate events, etc. We offer services that are completely unique to the personality, style and taste of our clients.

We take pride in our work giving 101% in everything we do, therefore with our qualifications and excellent coordination, creativity and planning skills, we will help you achieve your event goals within your budget.
